We are a 72-year-old couple, blessed to be married for 52 years to each other, one of the few things in our fleshly life that we can review with satisfaction. I am Hall V. Worthington and my wife is Joan Worthington. 

We are not reverend, father, brother, sister, doctor, pastor, or priest. Without title, we are simply Hall and Joan, like Nathaniel, Peter, Aquila, Priscilla and George Fox — drawn in their trades by God to be taught by, changed by, and serve as commanded by the captain of our salvation, the Lord Jesus Christ.

This basic form of this web site first appeared in 4/06. We scanned Fox's works from the 1831 edition of The Works of George Fox; we then corrected the scans, updated the language, and wrote the home page plus the From the Editors pages. We have attempted to update the language on his Journal, Letters, and Doctrinal Books so that they can be read 200 years from now. Lately we have added works from Margaret Fox, Isaac Penington, Francis Howgill, William Penn and many other eminent early Quakers, plus historical background information. Whatever wisdom there may be in this site is to the glory of Jesus Christ and the Father. We apologize for any typographical or grammar errors not yet corrected.

We were silent for twenty-five years while being taught and changed, before receiving His explicit command in January 06, to publish this web site, which includes His frequent specific words and corrections to our words. The site's purpose is to show you the true hope and inspire your much faster journey to purity and the Kingdom of God within you. We have struggled, made many mistakes, wandered through many deficit sects of "christendom," been wrong and corrected, been wrong and corrected again — but always encouraged, strengthened, and rewarded by Christ for our efforts and progress. Our knowledge comes from the Bible, George Fox's writings, and direct teachings of Christ through the Holy Spirit of God; everything was given to us by Him. We happily report we did not go to Bible College to learn. We do not ask for contributions, for Jesus said, "Freely you have received, freely give;" which both Peter and Paul echoed as forbidden. We are not recruiting for any organization. We are not affiliated with any of the sects or religions. Our motivation is to be obedient to our Lord's commands and our love for all our fellow men. We also look forward to seeing the world change; when the few, who can and were meant to hear, enter the Kingdom and walk in the spirit and power of God.

For those who can hear the message of this web site, be sure to read How to Benefit from the Changing Power of God. Grace will teach you what to deny and how to live soberly, godly, and righteously in this present world; you need no other teacher than the anointing within you — the light and word Christ. 1 John 2:27.

Jesus said: "There is only one thing needed. Mary has chosen that good part, which shall not be taken away from her" —
to sit at the feet of Jesus and hear his word; the words that I speak to you are spirit, and they are life, Luke 10:39-42, John 6:63.
Everyone who is of the truth hears my voice. My sheep hear my voice. I know them, and they follow me. John 18:37,10:27.

It is a cross to a man's will to sit in humble silence to wait on God, listening, and watching, while struggling to keep his mind from wandering; it is a cross to a man's will to obey what he is commanded and taught by the Holy Spirit; this is the cross of which you must deny yourself and pick it up every day, to then follow Jesus. To obey his voice is to follow Jesus. This is how to do what you have prayed for a thousand times: thy will be done, on earth as it is in heaven. You cannot be his disciple unless you pick up your cross daily and follow his orders. Salvation is only received by those who follow the lamb wherever he goes — who obey completely the voice of the Lord, in whatever he commands. Do not be discouraged if you don't hear for some time. Even your waiting is to your credit: Let your mercy, O LORD, be upon us, in proportion to our waiting and hoping on you. Psalms 33:22

If you listen and obey daily, HE will lead you out of the darkness that you may not sin. Whatever commands you receive and hear, continually lay them to heart, and obey them and repent. Rev 3:3. Keep a notebook of commands and understandings received. He told us, "Why should I teach you if you don't write down what I tell you?" For building your hope, read George Fox's and other early Quakers' Journals and Letters, and this site's on-line Bible, available with commentary to understand the true hope; and keep seeking while on the cross until you run into the arms of Jesus. We give you our love, our prayers, and our wish for your peace. Regarding the validity of purity through the cross: 1) there is a page with links to some of the most inspirational testimonies of the early Quakers, and 2) our lesser testimony is also available. For those curious, there is also a record of our journey of seeking. If you have sincere questions about the subjects on this site, email us, (but we don't open attachments); we will try with God's assistance to help you.

For those who think we are evil, stupid, or who feel threatened by the message of this site:

First, We sympathize with you. To some of you this is shocking and attacks what you hold to be your sacred beliefs. We too were told by many "experts" that there was nothing to do other than accept Jesus; confess that Jesus is Lord; get baptized; according to some, receive the Holy Spirit; go to services; and study the Bible for growth.

But, after all of the above and more, I still had problems: like anger and lust. And, no matter how many times I was 'saved,' I failed to measure up to Christ's admonitions, among them: Be perfect, therefore, as your Fatherwho is in heaven is perfect, Mat 5:48, and anyone who looks at a woman lustfully has already committed adultery with her in his heart, (Mat 5:28). All of the Bible's warnings, the requirements, and qualifying conditions were being ignored by Christendom. And I could see leading Buddhists and Hindus who were far more peaceful, gentle, and godly than any of the living leaders in Christendom. But I knew Jesus was the right way; his teachings and record were so far superior to the founders of all other faiths. So there was something very wrong, something that everyone in Christendom was missing. We knew the early church was full of miracles and holy, perfect men with the power of God for miracles that healed entire towns; but what happened? So I started researching Christian history, and stumbled across the early Quaker writings; and then to the founder of the Quakers, George Fox. And there it was again. The power of Paul, the power of Peter, in many thousands of early Quakers, (modern Quakers are radically different). So, holiness was and is possible; power was and is possible. The Kingdom of Heaven can be entered while on earth. Their record is in this site. You can read it too. It is free. It is a click away.

Second, Click here to see a list of Jesus' recorded commands from the Bible. Now see how many you can keep or obey — such as love your enemies, turn the other cheek, love your neighbor as yourself, be perfect as the Father is perfect, be merciful as your Father also is merciful. Ask yourself, why would Jesus have wasted his breath giving us all these commands unless he was showing us a way to keep them? Look for the way. It is going to Jesus personally to learn from Him, get cleansed by Him, led by Him, and come to know Him. (See Jesus Wants to Teach You Himself.) My sheep hear My voice, and I know them, and they follow Me. John 10:27.  If you abide in My word, you are truly My disciples. You will know the truth, and the truth will set you free. John 8:31-32. Everyone who is of the truth hears My voice. John 18:37

The hope of purity, and the cross as the means to achieve it, are what is missing in the deficit sects today. This site shows the way to the cross: to persistently and patiently sit in humble silence while waiting on God, as you listen and watch for the Lord your teacher each day, repenting of the conditions he shows you in your heart, and following his commands that you hear Jesus speak directly to you — all of these are a cross to your will. As Jesus said: For everyone who comes to Me and listens to My spoken words and does them lays the sure foundation of true faith that survives. You have found the way to salvation, which is to be freed from even the desire to sin. As Paul said in Philippians, "continue to work out your salvation with fear and trembling." He cleanses us, to purify our hearts and souls. Grace through loving, obedient faith in Jesus Christ is what changes you, as you pick up your cross daily and lose your life to save it; but grace is not a license to continue to sin. Grace does not grant instant salvation; it is a teaching process to change you, destroying your sinful nature through the cross — resulting in the fruit of the Spirit: love, peace, joy, gentleness, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, etc.

And whoever does not bear his cross and come after me cannot be my disciple.
Luke 14:27

Those who belong to Christ have crucified the flesh [the sinful nature] with its passions and desires [lusts and affections].
Gal 5:24

When you bear (produce or bring forth) much fruit, My Father is honored and glorified,
and you show that you are truly my disciples [true followers].
John 15:8

Third, Don't depend on any man or writing for your salvation; there are millions of blind guides on the broad way that are in the ditch, (you will know them by their fruits); you have no need of any teacher other than the anointing within you.  Don't rely on what others tell you what the Bible says; read the New Testament yourself. Notice the many statements requiring holiness, purity, righteousness, killing the sinful nature or fleshly self, perfection; notice the many disqualifications for anyone who continues to live in sin or in the flesh: immoral, impure, greedy, drunken, given to rage, envious, following pleasures, selfish ambition, jealous, lying, hatred, discord, partying, pride, etc. Then Pray for guidance. But remember, to get guidance, you have to be quiet and listen for an answer. Then you can make your own decisions about what is required of you. Our love and peace to you. And may you hear directly from the God of Peace, our Lord Jesus Christ, to guide you to his and the Father's will.

From lovers of your soul,

Hall Worthington and Joan Worthington

Regarding the name of the web site being my name, with middle initial:

Back in Jan 06, I was told by the Lord while waiting on Him to put up a web site in my name, including the middle initial. I put up a personal information web site, in my name without the middle initial: www.hallworthington.com. The Lord told me I had not done as he had commanded. We had included a link to an abbreviated George Fox's Journal at another site; and since I loved Fox's letters so much, I began to scan and upload his letters to the site in a separate link. When we discovered that the link to his Journal was to an abbreviated version, we started scanning his full, unabbreviated Journal and uploading it too. The site became 99% George Fox's writings, and so I decided to change the name of the web site to www.georgefoxwritings.com, thinking it more accurately reflected the site's content, and giving credit where credit was due; the Lord told me afterwards: "you still have not named the web site as I commanded you." I was reluctant to do so because it seemed so egotistical.

Shortly after completely uploading Fox's Letters and Journal, we started adding other authors we had found. So, this time I changed the web site name to www.hallvworthington.com as he had commanded and reminded me of my failure to obey twice. It remains to be seen why the Lord felt it necessary to use my name on the web site, but I am sure it will become clear in the near future.
